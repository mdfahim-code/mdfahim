Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical process for a project?
My process starts with understanding your goals through an initial consultation. Then, I move onto wireframing and design (if required). Once the design is approved, I build the project keeping scalability and clean code in mind. We finish with testing and deployment.
What if I need changes after the project is done?
Each pricing tier includes a specific number of revisions and a free support window. For changes outside this scope, I offer an hourly support rate or a retainer package tailored to your ongoing needs.
Do you provide hosting and domains?
I assist you in setting them up to your preferred platforms like Vercel, Netlify, or AWS for frontend/backend hosting, ensuring optimal performance for your site. The Enterprise package includes full deployment setup for you.
How long does a website take to build?
The timeline depends heavily on the project scope. A standard landing page might take less than a week, while a complex, multi-page application with backend logic could take 3-6 weeks. I provide a dedicated timeline during our initial brief.
